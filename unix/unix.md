Some common formats:

- `%A, %B %e, %Y`: Sunday, June 5, 2015
- `%b %e %a`: Jun 5 Sun

Date formats

- `%a`: sun
- `%A`: Sunday
- `%w`: weekday number (0 - 6)
- `%y`: 13
- `%Y`: 2013
- `%b`: Jan
- `%B`: January
- `%m`: month number (01 - 12)
- `%d`: day number in month (01 - 31)
- `%e`: day number in month (int formatted) (1 - 31)

Time formats

- `%l`: Hour (int formatted) (1 - 24)
- `%H`: 24h Hour (00 - 23)
- `%I`: 12h Hour (01 - 12)
- `%M`: Minutes (00 - 59)
- `%S`: Seconds (00 - 60)
- `%p`: AM or PM
- `%Z`: Timezone (ex: +08)
- `%j`: Day of the year (001 - 366)
- `%%`: Litteral `%` character
